# User Guide (Citizens, Students, Business Owners, Investors)

1. **Register:** Go to *Create Account*, choose your account type, and submit the form. Citizen
   and Student accounts are auto-approved for this demo; other types can be gated behind admin
   approval (`isApproved` field) in a production deployment.
2. **Login:** Use your email and password. Your session persists via a secure cookie.
3. **Dashboard:** After logging in, `/[locale]/dashboard` shows your digital ID card & QR code,
   recent requests, appointments, documents, and notifications.
4. **Submit a request:** From the dashboard, click *New Request*, choose a type (lost passport,
   residency issue, business issue, etc.), describe your case, and submit. You'll receive a
   reference number immediately.
5. **Book an appointment:** Go to *Appointments*, pick an open time slot, and confirm. Slots show
   live capacity so you never double-book a full slot.
6. **Emergency:** Go to *Emergency*, select a case type, describe the situation, and submit. This
   creates an internal urgent ticket — for immediate life-threatening danger, always call local
   emergency numbers directly (Police 122 / Ambulance 123 in Egypt) in addition to using this form.
7. **Students:** The Student Portal lists partner universities, scholarships, internships, jobs,
   and cultural events.
8. **Business/Investment:** Browse opportunities by sector/country; use *New Request* to express
   interest — demo data here is illustrative and not a binding offer.
9. **Directory:** Browse verified places in Alexandria (hospitals, universities, banks, etc.) by
   category.
10. **Translate:** Use the Translation Center for quick text translation — remember it is not a
    substitute for certified translation of official documents.
